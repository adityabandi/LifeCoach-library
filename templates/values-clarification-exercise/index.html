VALUES CLARIFICATION EXERCISE INTRODUCTION ============ Your values are your core beliefs and principles that guide your decisions and behavior. When your life and goals align with your values, you experience greater satisfaction, motivation, and authenticity. This exercise will help you identify and prioritize your most important values.
