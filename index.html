Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your life coaching journey

What is life coaching?

Life coaching is a collaborative process where a trained professional helps individuals identify and achieve personal and professional goals through guidance, support, and accountability. Unlike therapy, which often focuses on healing past wounds, life coaching is forward-focused and action-oriented.

How do I start a life coaching business?

Starting a life coaching business involves getting certified, defining your niche, creating a business plan, building your brand, and developing marketing strategies to attract clients. Our complete guide covers each step with actionable templates and proven strategies.

What templates do life coaches need?

Life coaches need intake forms, session notes templates, goal-setting worksheets, progress tracking tools, coaching agreements, and marketing materials. We provide all these templates free of charge, ready to use immediately.

How much should I charge for life coaching?

Life coaching rates vary based on experience, location, and niche. New coaches typically charge $50-150 per session, while experienced coaches may charge $200-500+ per session. Our pricing guide helps you set competitive rates that reflect your value.
